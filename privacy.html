Me.

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2026

Last updated: 26 May 2026

Me. is developed by Osborne Images Pty Ltd, based in Tasmania, Australia.

This Privacy Policy explains how Me. handles your information.

Me. is designed as a local-first application. The data you create in Me. — check-ins, notes, annotations, your Health Profile, saved prompts, and compilations — lives on your device, or in your own iCloud account if you choose to turn on sync.

We do not run an account system, and we do not store your health data on our servers. Your personal health information is never transmitted to Oi Studios. The only information that can ever reach us is an anonymous prompt you explicitly choose to share, which contains no health data or personal information (see Anonymous prompt sharing below).

Information Me. accesses

With your permission, Me. may access health and wellness information stored in Apple Health, including data such as:

  • Sleep
  • Heart rate
  • Activity
  • Workouts
  • Recovery metrics
  • Mindfulness sessions
  • Other health categories you explicitly allow through Apple Health permissions

Me. only accesses the categories you approve through Apple's HealthKit permission system.

You can revoke Health permissions at any time through:

iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Me.

Apple Health data

Health data accessed through Apple Health is used solely to display, organise, and compile information within Me.

HealthKit data is never used for advertising, marketing, profiling, or behavioural tracking purposes.

Me. does not sell HealthKit data or share HealthKit data with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing platforms.

Health data remains under your control through Apple's permission system and your device settings.

Personal entries and annotations

Me. allows you to create:

  • Check-ins
  • Notes
  • Voice-transcribed annotations
  • Health Profile entries
  • Structured compilations and exports

These entries are stored locally on your device. If you turn on iCloud sync, they also sync through your own iCloud account (see iCloud sync below). They are not sent to Oi Studios unless you choose to share an anonymous prompt.

You are responsible for any information you choose to include in notes, exports, or AI prompts generated from Me.

iCloud sync

Me. offers optional iCloud sync. When you turn it on, the data you create in Me. syncs through your own iCloud account so it stays available across your devices and after reinstalling the app.

This data syncs to your personal iCloud account, not to Oi Studios. We have no access to it. Apple acts as the data processor for iCloud, subject to Apple's own terms and privacy policy.

Your raw Apple Health data is never synced by Me. Each device reads Apple Health directly through HealthKit.

iCloud sync is off until you turn it on, and you can turn it off at any time in Settings.

Voice input

Me. uses Apple's Speech framework for voice-to-text transcription.

Me. is designed to use on-device speech recognition where supported by Apple and the user's device configuration. Speech processing behaviour may vary depending on iOS version, language support, and Apple system settings.

Me. does not transmit or store voice recordings on our servers. Transcribed text is stored locally on your device as part of your annotations.

Sharing and exports

Me. allows you to export or share compilations and structured outputs with:

  • Healthcare providers
  • Coaches
  • Family members
  • Third-party AI systems
  • Other apps or services you choose

Once information leaves Me., its handling becomes subject to the privacy practices and policies of the receiving service or person.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party service before sharing health information.

Anonymous prompt sharing

Me. includes an optional way to contribute a prompt you have written to a shared library that helps improve prompt suggestions for other people using Me.

If you choose to share a prompt, only the prompt text is transmitted. No health data, compile content, annotations, profile information, or any identifier is included. The submission is anonymous and cannot be traced back to you.

Sharing is off by default and must be turned on each time you want to share a prompt. It does not stay on between sessions. You are never required to share anything.

AI tools and third-party services

Me. may be used alongside third-party AI systems such as ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

When you copy, export, or paste information into external AI systems, those services handle the data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

Me. does not control, monitor, or take responsibility for how third-party AI services process shared information.

Analytics

Me. uses TelemetryDeck for privacy-focused anonymous usage analytics.

TelemetryDeck is configured to avoid collecting directly identifying personal information or health data. Analytics may include limited technical and usage information such as feature usage, screen visits, app version, and general device information used to improve app stability and usability.

No advertising identifiers, third-party advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking technologies are used.

You can disable anonymous analytics at any time within the app settings.

Subscription and payment information

Subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store.

We do not receive or store your payment card information.

Apple may provide limited subscription-related information to confirm subscription status and manage purchases.

Subscription billing and payment processing are governed by Apple's terms and privacy policies.

Data storage and security

Me. stores your data locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data also resides in your own iCloud account, secured by Apple.

Security protections depend partly on your own device security settings, including passcodes, biometric authentication, iCloud configuration, and device encryption.

While we aim to use reasonable security practices, no software or device environment can be guaranteed completely secure.

Data deletion

Because Me. stores data locally on your device, deleting the app generally removes Me. data stored on the device.

If you have enabled iCloud sync, your data also exists in your iCloud account. You can manage or delete this data through your device's iCloud settings. App data may also exist within Apple-managed device backups, including iCloud backups, depending on your device settings. Any exported files or shared compilations created outside the app remain your responsibility.

Me. does not maintain user accounts or server-side health data records requiring separate deletion requests.

Children's privacy

Me. is not intended for children under 18 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Future services

If future versions of Me. introduce new optional features that change how data is handled, such as user accounts or new online services, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features become available.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updated versions will be published at meapp.health/privacy.

Continued use of Me. after updates become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Where practical, material changes may also be noted within the app or release notes.
